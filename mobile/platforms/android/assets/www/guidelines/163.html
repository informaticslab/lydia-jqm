Recommended Evaluation

No evaluation is required.

Recommended Regimen - Infants who have a normal physical examination and a serum quantitative nontreponemal serologic titer the same or less than fourfold the maternal titer and the 1. mother was treated during pregnancy, treatment was appropriate for the stage of infection, and treatment was administered >4 weeks before delivery and 2. mother has no evidence of reinfection or relapse.
Benzathine penicillin G 50,000 units/kg/dose IM in a single dose*
